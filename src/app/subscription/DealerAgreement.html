Services Provided by Factory KeyAssure, Factory KeyAssure agrees to provide services to Dealer Members through its website (www.KeyAssure.live) and mobile application by providing the dealer with an appraisal tool, wholesale marketplace, inventory management tool and by providing dealers with KeyAssure OBD Tool.

2. The Mobile Application and Web base. The application is the sole property of Factory KeyAssure. Factory KeyAssure grants permission to the Dealer Members and its authorized employees to use the application. These employees may install the application on their personal devices, or the devices owned by the Dealer Member. The Member Dealer and its authorized employees shall agree to delete the application if they should leave the employment of the Dealer Member or if the dealer member relinquishes its membership in Factory KeyAssure. The Members agree to protect the application from 3rd party duplication.

3. Membership Dues paid by Dealer. Dealer shall pay to Factory KeyAssure monthly membership dues in the amount of $599 ("Membership Dues") for Factory KeyAssure’s services. Membership Dues shall be paid prior to the first day of the month in which Factory KeyAssure shall provide its services.

4. Representations by Dealer. Dealer specifically represents that it is a business lawfully licensed in the states in which it conducts business and that it is a state licensed automotive Dealer. Dealer shall provide to Factory KeyAssure current copies of any licenses and Federal Tax Certificate required by the states in which the Dealer conducts business.

This data is solely the responsibility of the 3 rd party provider. Dealer members shall hold Factory KeyAssure harmless as to the nature and accuracy of this 3rd party data.

6. Virtual Cloud Base Wholesale Marketplace. Factory KeyAssure agrees to list all the Dealer Members preowned vehicles that have been appraised by Factory KeyAssure appraisal tool on Factory KeyAssure cloud database free of charge to the Dealer Member. Factory KeyAssure shall provide for the use of the dealer member a buy figure request system that petitions a buy figure from dealer members who have specifically requested vehicles that match the vehicle being appraised.

7. Buying and Selling a Vehicle to and From a Factory KeyAssure Dealer Member. The Dealer Member may at its discretion elect to sell (Wholesale) a pre-owned vehicle to another Factory KeyAssure Dealer Member, by accepting the dealer member’s offer. The Dealer Member may also elect to buy (Wholesale) or sell (Retail) to Members of the Public that list their Vehicles on the Factory KeyAssure Network or Contact the Dealer to buy though Factory KeyAssure Network. The Dealer Member agrees to pay Factory KeyAssure a sell fee according to the following fee schedule: Buy Fee: $200.00 Sell Fee : $200.00 The agreement to Purchase/Sell will not become binding or the fee charged until the selling Dealer accepts the Members offer OR the buying Dealer Member accepts the counter offer of the selling Dealer Member or Public Member

8. Refunds. The Factory offers no refunds or exchanges of any kind. Once both Factory Dealer Members have agreed to a transaction, we disclose information that cannot be undisclosed. Therefore, no refund can or will be made. The Sole Exception to this rule is during arbitration. As a result of the arbitration process Factory KeyAssure may refund all funds up to and including buy and sell fees.

9. Guidelines of Member Conduct. The Dealer Member agrees to conduct itself and its authorized employees by Factory KeyAssure guidelines regarding wholesale and Retail transactions through the Factory KeyAssure Platform, both the mobile application and web base.

The guidelines are as follows:

Dealer Member agree to use the application to appraise the condition of all pre-owned vehicles. The value a Dealer Member places on each pre-owned vehicle is at the sole discretion of the Dealer Member and will not be displayed for other Factory KeyAssure Dealer Members. The Dealer Member agrees that it and its authorized employees shall assess the condition honestly and professionally and shall not falsify or mislead as to the condition of a vehicle. The Dealer Member agrees to list all pre-owned inventory on the Factory KeyAssure Platform/Network. The Dealer Member agrees to honor all offers to purchase by its authorized employees. Offers on inventory vehicles are valid for 24 hours, or until that are accepted, declined, or countered, whichever comes first. A Counteroffer is binding on the Seller for 24 hours or until it is declined or accepted whichever comes first. Offers on trade appraisal buy figure request (buy figures) are binding for 72 hours. A Buy Figure Counteroffer is binding on the seller for 72 hours, and only if the vehicle trade is taken into stock. The Dealer Member are responsible for assigning employees authorized to purchase or accept an offer. The Dealer Member agrees to pay Factory KeyAssure the fees spelled out in section 7 of this agreement, for any Sales or Purchases through the Factory KeyAssure Platform Network. The Dealer Member agrees to place on file a credit card or ACH with Factory KeyAssure for future Purchases or Sales within The Factory Platform Network. The Factory shall charge the credit card or ACH on file ONLY when an offer is accepted by the Seller OR when the buyer accepts the counteroffer using The Factory Platform Network. The Dealer Members agree to pay for the agreed upon offer that was accepted by a selling Dealer Member. The Buying Dealer is responsible for arranging and the cost of transportation.

10. Disputes. Disputes between Dealer Members should be worked out equitably between the Dealer Members. If a resolution cannot be reached, members agree to arbitration by Factory KeyAssure Personnel. Factory KeyAssure’s arbitration shall be final.

11. Arbitration. This dispute resolution is a service that Factory KeyAssure offers as an added protection for both Buyers and Sellers to assure fair and ethical sales. If section 14 "Disputes" in this agreement cannot be resolved between buyer and seller, Factory KeyAssure will become involved in arbitration. During this process, Factory KeyAssure will act as a neutral intermediary to investigate and resolve on behalf of both parties all transaction related disputes. Outcomes could include sale price adjustment or sales cancellation. Arbitration process: l) Dispute between parties were attempted without success. 2) Vehicle is eligible to arbitration under the terms. 3) An on-line claim form through Factory KeyAssure Web base portal must be filled out from the buyer within 72 hours of buyer having possession of the vehicle. 4) Factory KeyAssure has 3 Business Days from the on-line claim filing to resolve or make final decision on dispute. Terms for arbitration: 1) The vehicle was not in the condition that was described by the seller in the appraisal documentation. Under the NAAA Arbitration Policy under process: 2) Any single mechanical defect that has a repair cost of $500 or more is subject to arbitration on vehicles 3) All vehicles sold for $3000 and under with a completed detail professional appraisal can only be arbitrated for the following reasons: A) Odometer issues (Present and Past). B) Title issues (Present and Past). No arbitration fees will be charged to either party by Factory KeyAssure for the service of intermediary.
